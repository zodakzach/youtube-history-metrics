How To Download Your YouTube Watch History?

Step 1: Navigate to takeout.google.com.
https://takeout.google.com
Hello!
Step 2: Select data for new export
  1. Log in to your Google account that is linked to your YouTube if not logged in already.
  2. Create a new export after logging in.
  3. Deselect all items and scroll down to find the "YouTube and YouTube Music" option.
  4. Select "YouTube and YouTube Music".
  5. Choose "Deselect all" under content options.
  6. Select the "History" option and click 'Ok'.
  7. Change the export format from HTML to JSON.
  8. Click on the format option.
  9. Choose JSON format and click 'Ok'.
  10. Proceed to the next step.
Step 3: Create export
  1. Click on the 'Create export' button.
  2. Wait for the export to finish. It usually takes about 5 minutes if only YouTube history is selected.
  3. Check your email for the download link.
  4. Download the data from the provided link and unzip the folder.
  5. Now you can analyze your YouTube watch history data as needed.
