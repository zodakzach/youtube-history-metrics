How To Download Your YouTube Watch History?

To analyze your watch history you must download it as a JSON file from google.

Follow steps below to do so.

https://takeout.google.com

Step 1: Navigate to takeout.google.com

This website allows you to export and download your Google data. Login with your google account linked to your YouTube and create a new export. However, you only need to export your YouTube watch history data.

Step 2 will guide you through the process of selecting this specific data.

Step 2: Select data for new export

  • Deselect all options by clicking 'Deselect all'.
  • Then scroll down and select the 'YouTube and YouTube Music' option shown to the right.
  • Click the format option and scroll down to history. Change format from HTML to JSON.
  • Click the content option and deselect all options except the history option.

Proceed to next step.

Step 3: Export and Download Data

  • Now that you have selected the data, you can leave the default export settings alone and click 'Create export'.
  • If you selected only the YouTube watch history data it should only take about 5 minutes to receive the email with the download link.
  • Download the takeout zip file and unzip it.

You are now ready to analyze your YouTube History Metrics.
